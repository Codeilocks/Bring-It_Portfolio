Security

RBAC

Role Based Access Control System (RBAC) was used. It enabled us to grant user-specific permissions and restrict access to the robot's compartments. The compartments only unlock when the correct RFID tag is scanned. Every RFID tag has its own user, and the users are assigned to a group. For peer to peer orders the RFID tags are assigned to groups instead. Admin is the superuser with all permissions. An order may only be edited or deleted only by the technician. Equipment can be ordered by nurses and doctors. Except for the admin, none of the users have access to the personal information of other employees. The RBAC system has been integrated with the User Interface sub-system.

Storage

The drawers are automatically opened when the authorized RFID tag is scanned. First the servo, pulls the spring of the lock mechanism, and then the linear actuator pulls the drawer. The lock mechanism has a spring in it that enabled us to modify its diameter, pitch and its length. The video of the system working can be accessed from the Training Material page on the portfolio. The RFID reader, servo, linear actuator has been integrated with the Robot under the Robot communication repository.
