Throughout the development and build stages we have been keeping a test plan which details each of our tests and which requirements they verify.

Capability:

Provided a secure internet connection is available, the robot takes orders to pickup/dropoff.

The robot's SLAM feature allows it to navigate around an environment with no moving obstacles and plenty of extractable features.

With no doors in the way, it can map a path to a goal within the map.

Using a LiDAR sensor, the robot can detect obstacles with 360 degrees at a height of 70cm

Via locked, automated drawers, it is capable of safely storing equipment
